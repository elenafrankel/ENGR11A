About Me!

Hi there! I am a fourth year undergrad studying math and physics at Brandeis University. Welcome to my website! I am currently taking Engineering 11A, and this website is where I will keep all the write-ups and information about my projects for the Spring 2025 semester. Happy to have you along for the ride!

What is Engineering 11A?

Engineering 11A was the first engineering class to be offered at Brandeis, starting in the Fall of 2023. It is an introduction to the concept of engineering through hands-on experimentation and teamwork. Failures are learning experiences here! As I progress through the semester, I will update this website with pictures of my successes and failures, and write-ups analyzing my processes.

My Interests in Engineering

I have always loved building and problem solving. In middle school I took my first “shop” class, where I learned how to design something to solve a problem, and then build it with the tools and resources available to me. My love of problem solving inspired me to pursue a degree in math, and my desire to effect real-world solutions fueled my addition of a degree in physics. I am thrilled to have the opportunity to take an engineering class in my last semester of my undergrad, and hope you enjoy looking around my website!
